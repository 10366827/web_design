Testimonials

The Moorings House B&B Galway

The Moorings B&B is a unique Bed & Breakfast located in the heart of Galway city. Our most recent awards for excellency come are the Tripadvisor2015 and Tripadvisor2016 awards. Our business is family run, and has been in operation for the past 20 years. We offer spectacular views over Galway Bay, and do our utmost to provide the best service. We're well established in the local town, and have connections with our local tourist guides. We're more than happy to advise on local activities to take part in, and attractions to see.

Warm hospitality awaits each guest. Guests will feel welcome in this lovely, comfortable home, making each of our guest rooms a delightful, pleasurable retreat. Each spacious guest room has a unique ambiance and is thoughtfully designed for your comfort and quiet relaxation.
